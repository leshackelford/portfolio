Collections

This project addressed a highly requested feature from our largest customers: the ability to purchase and allocate bandwidth to specific projects. Additionally, companies with multiple departments needed separate "collections" for dedicated bandwidth, proxy configuration, and usage data. I collaborated with every department in the company to develop this feature. Using Mockflow, I designed the UI for collections alongside the CEO, Project Manager, and VP of Tech. I then partnered with the lead developer to write the user stories, equipping the development team with the necessary tools to execute the project. The team successfully completed the project in February 2024.
