Newsletter Content Management Process

During my time at Open Door Mission, I created and maintained the content management process for all digital media. This started with the monthly newsletter. While this was print, the interviews that ended up on the newsletter would be distributed to email, social media, blog & website.

  1. Work with Case Managers to Identify Someone to Interview
  2. Interview
  3. Take Photos
  4. Send Info to Content Writer
  5. Make Needed Edits
  6. Publish on all digital media for ODM
