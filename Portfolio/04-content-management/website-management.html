Webiste Management

At Sprious (Rayobyte) we operated by experiments in 2-week increments. One of those experiments were brand websites. If we posted proxy content via a series of different brand websites, we predicted we would be able to bring in extra revenue and would be worth the company's time.

I would build websites from scratch like, proxyreseller.com and manage internal teammembers or hire freelancers to write content for these websites.

This was a very successful experiment as the time put in versus revenue output was about 20 hours for $10,000 per month.

With that success, I would continue experimenting on the type of freelancer, i.e. Fiverr vs. Upwork. I learned that any Fiverr contractor I had hired would write content I would ultimately need to re-write, so it made sense for us to utilize more expensive writers.

Another adjustment made was number of websites. We'd started with 6, but only ended up keeping up with 3 as the other 3 had not generated enough revenue to keep up on.
