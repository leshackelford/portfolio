Blog Management Process

During my time as a Brand Manager at Sprious (Rayobyte), one of my main functions included complete management of the website's extensive blog.

I helped build and maintain a content process machine that published 50,000 words a month, 5+ blogs a week. An (over-simplified) breakdown of that process:

  1. Industry Trends & Research
  2. Keyword Research
  3. SEO Research
  4. Build Out Content for Articles + Main Points
  5. Send Info to Content Writers
  6. Make Needed Edits
  7. Manage Freelancers in publishing content and designing graphics
  8. Publish on Website
