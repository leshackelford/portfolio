Omaha Gives

Each year, the city of Omaha would host a day of giving for the areas registered 501c(3)s. For Open Door Mission, this was always one of the biggest giving days of the year and required a LOT of prep work. These projects were probably some of my favorite I ever worked on as I was able to create a vision, hear some amazing stories, implement the vision based on said stories and gained invaluable experience.
