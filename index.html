About Me

Eight years of marketing in a number of industries has given me the opportunity to "try it all". Think social media, video, photography, writing, paid ads, SEO, email marketing, billboards, print materials, banners, postcards/various forms of mailers, website management, re-branding, campaign management, content management, internal and external communications.. The list goes on.

Because of this extensive experience, I have often had interviewers, other marketers and even friends ask me what part of marketing I love the most.

If I can give an honest answer, none of the marketing disciplines stick out.

What sticks out to me are the people and stories I have been fortunate enough to encounter along the way. Those shared experiences and moments I would otherwise never would have had. I think of an old boss I would meet for breakfast and hear his background at the IRS in Washington D.C. and his path back to Nebraska. I think of the man who was the third generation of family to have an addiciton starting around the age of 10 - and him being the first in his family to seek help. I think of the re-branding from Blazing SEO to Rayoibyte and the countless hours we put into our brand communicating exactly who we were and what we wanted to offer our customers.

Marketing, to me, is a tool for a business to create honest, meaningful interactions with the world.

Therefore, I live by 3 basic, but powerful marketing principles:

Work Hard. Market Honest. Tell More Stories.
