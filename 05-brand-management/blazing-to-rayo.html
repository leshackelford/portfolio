Rebranding: Blazing SEO to Rayobyte

  • Company Rebranding
  • Product Rebranding
  • Delegation
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration

When I was first hired on as the Brand Manager for Blazing SEO, my first big challenge was to understand Sprious' positive intention for naming their biggest product a name that had little to do with proxies. I learned that using proxies for SEO was a much bigger use case back when the company was started in 2014, but had lost relevance over the years. With that, I asked if we could consider re-branding to something new. The leadership team ultimately agreed and the rebranding project was born. With the help of my boss and the CEO, we iterated through and picked a new name, Rayobyte, created a branding story on why we'd made the decision to change our name, and updated every single piece of the company that had any Blazing SEO labeling. There was a lot of inter-departmental collaboration as this project touched everyone in the company.

This re-branding project led to a 50% increase in weekly traffic for the Rayobyte website.
